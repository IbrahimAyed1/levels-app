01 — About

We make coffee the way we'd
want it made for us.

Levels began as a small bar on a quiet street in Qurum — a single machine, a hand grinder, and a stubborn idea about what a flat white should taste like.

Seven years on, the idea hasn't changed. We roast in small batches, we don't chase trends, and we'd rather get one cup right than serve a hundred we're not proud of. The room is quiet on purpose. The menu is long because every item earns its place.

We work with five farms across Ethiopia, Kenya and Yemen. Beans are roasted Tuesday, rested Wednesday, on the bar by Thursday — never older than ten days when we serve them.

05 — The Levels Family

More than regulars. Family.

The Levels Family is for the people who treat us like a second kitchen — who know the staff's names and bring their kids and their grandparents and their first dates. Members get early access to seasonal menus, invitations to tasting evenings, and a monthly bag of beans calibrated to your palate. Interest list opening soon — leave your email and we'll reach out.
